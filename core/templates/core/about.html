About

Bibliotype is a free tool that analyzes your reading history to reveal your unique Reading DNA — a personalized profile of your literary personality, favorite genres, reading habits, and book preferences.

How It Works

  1. Export your library from Goodreads as a CSV file.
  2. Upload the file to Bibliotype.
  3. Get your personalized Reading DNA dashboard with insights into your literary personality type, reading statistics, genre breakdowns, and AI-generated reading vibes.

What You Get

  • Reader Type — your literary personality classification based on what you read
  • Reading Vibe — AI-generated phrases that capture the essence of your reading taste
  • Genre & Author Insights — breakdowns of your most-read genres and favorite authors
  • Reading Statistics — page counts, ratings analysis, reading pace, and yearly trends
  • Community Benchmarking — see how your reading compares to the Bibliotype community
  • Book Recommendations — personalized suggestions based on readers with similar taste

Your Data

Anonymous uploads are processed temporarily and deleted after 7 days. Create a free account to save your Bibliotype permanently and share it with friends. Read our Privacy Policy for full details on how we handle your data.

Open Source

Bibliotype is open source. You can find the code on GitHub.
