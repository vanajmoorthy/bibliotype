About

What is Bibliotype?

Bibliotype is a free tool that analyses your reading history to reveal your unique Reading DNA, a personalised profile of your literary personality, favourite genres, reading habits, and book preferences.

Upload your Goodreads export and you'll get back a dashboard packed with insights. You'll find out your reader type, a dedicated reading vibe, detailed genre and author breakdowns, reading statistics like page counts and yearly trends, community benchmarking to see how you compare to other readers, and personalised book recommendations based on people with similar taste.

Why did I build this?

I built Bibliotype because I wanted to see this kind of data about my own reading, and about my friends' reading too. Goodreads gives you a basic yearly summary but I wanted to know more: what kind of reader am I, really? What patterns show up when you look at hundreds of books together? How does my taste compare to other people?

It started as a fun side project and grew more convoluted from there. It's all open source, so if you're curious about how it works under the hood, feel free to have a look around.

How does it work?

  1. Export your library from Goodreads as a CSV file.
  2. Upload the file to Bibliotype.
  3. Get your personalised Bibliotype dashboard.

What do we do with your data?

Anonymous uploads are processed temporarily and deleted after 7 days. Create a free account to save your Bibliotype permanently and share it with friends. Read the Privacy Policy for full details on how your data is handled.
