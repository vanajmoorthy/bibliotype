About

Bibliotype is a free tool that analyses your reading history to reveal your unique Reading DNA — a personalised profile of your literary personality, favourite genres, reading habits, and book preferences.

Upload your Goodreads export and you'll get back a dashboard packed with insights: your reader type, an AI-generated reading vibe, detailed genre and author breakdowns, reading statistics like page counts and yearly trends, community benchmarking to see how you compare to other readers, and personalised book recommendations based on people with similar taste.

Why I Built This

I built Bibliotype because I wanted to see this kind of data about my own reading — and about my friends' reading too. Goodreads gives you a basic yearly summary, but I wanted something deeper: what kind of reader am I, really? What patterns show up when you look at hundreds of books together? How does my taste compare to other people?

It started as a fun side project and grew from there. The whole thing is open source, so if you're curious about how it works under the hood, have a look around.

How It Works

  1. Export your library from Goodreads as a CSV file.
  2. Upload the file to Bibliotype.
  3. Get your personalised Reading DNA dashboard.

Your Data

Anonymous uploads are processed temporarily and deleted after 7 days. Create a free account to save your Bibliotype permanently and share it with friends. Read the Privacy Policy for full details on how your data is handled.
